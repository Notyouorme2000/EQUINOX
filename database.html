// MANDATORY READING -- ALL PERSONNEL
RULES & GUIDELINES
This is a text-based roleplay server. All participants are expected to read and follow these directives before engaging. Ignorance is not an excuse. Violations are logged and actioned by staff.
// 01 General Conduct
R-001
Respect Everyone
Treat all members with basic respect both in and out of character. Targeted insults, personal attacks, and sustained hostility toward other players will not be tolerated regardless of in-character justification.
SERIOUS
R-002
No Metagaming
Your character only knows what they have learned through in-character interactions. Using out-of-character knowledge (such as information from other channels, DMs, or previous characters) to influence your RP is strictly prohibited.
SERIOUS
R-003
No Godmodding
You do not control other players' characters. Do not write actions that dictate what another character does, feels, or experiences without their consent. All combat and consequential actions must be collaborative.
SERIOUS
R-004
No Powerplaying
Characters must have realistic limitations. You cannot be invincible, all-knowing, or conveniently perfect. Play your character's weaknesses as seriously as their strengths. Unrealistic feats without staff approval will be voided.
SERIOUS
R-005
Don't Disrupt RP
Do not break immersion in active RP scenes. Keep out-of-character conversation to designated OOC channels. If you need to step out of a scene, use double brackets (( )) to indicate OOC communication.
SERIOUS
R-006
No ERP (Erotic Roleplay)
Explicit sexual content of any kind is strictly forbidden on this server. This includes implied content, suggestive scenes pushed past a kiss or a hug, or soliciting other players for such content is not allowed.
CRITICAL
R-007
No Harassment or Hate Speech
Racism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, or any other form of targeted hate speech, whether in-character or out, results in an immediate permanent ban.
CRITICAL
R-008
Follow Staff Directives
Staff decisions are final during active moderation. If you disagree with a ruling, raise it calmly through the appeals channel after the fact. Arguing with staff in public channels or during an active scene will be treated as misconduct.
SERIOUS
R-009
Respect Character Death and Consequences
If your character dies or suffers a major consequence through legitimate RP, accept it. Retconning deaths, injuries, or events without staff approval undermines the integrity of the server narrative and will be reversed.
SERIOUS
R-010
No Lore Violations
All roleplay must remain consistent with established SCP: EQUINOX lore. Do not introduce canon-breaking elements, unauthorized SCPs, or contradictory backstory without approval from the lore team.
SERIOUS
// 02 Text RP Standards
// WRITING FORMAT
Post Quality
Write in third person past tense. Put effort into your posts. One-liners in serious RP scenes are disrespectful to other writers. Minimum two sentences per action post is expected. Longer scenes deserve longer responses.
// CONSENT
Dark Themes
SCP lore involves heavy and disturbing themes. Any scene involving torture, psychological horror, or character trauma requires OOC consent from all involved players beforehand. Use the content warning system provided by staff.
// CHARACTERS
Character Approval
All new characters must be submitted to the character approval channel before use in active RP. High-clearance Foundation roles, anomalous characters, and faction leadership positions require additional staff sign-off.
// CONFLICT
Player vs Player
Capture, interrogation, combat, or elimination scenarios require mutual OOC agreement before they begin. Surprise hostile action against another player's character without prior consent is treated as godmodding.
// ESCALATION
Scene Escalation
If a scene is escalating in a direction you are uncomfortable with, you may call a pause using (( pause )) at any time. Other players must respect this immediately. Continuing a scene after a pause is called is a serious violation.
// OOC
Out of Character Channels
OOC channels exist for casual chat, questions, and coordination. Keep them constructive. Venting frustration about other players publicly, even without naming them, is considered indirect harassment and will be actioned.
